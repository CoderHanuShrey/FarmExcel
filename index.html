Effects of Less Organic Content In Soil

Failing soil is a serious global issue causing our food to be less and less nutritious year by year. Every year 27000 species of microbial life is going extinct, mainly because of lack of required organic content in the soil. Soil will become sand if its organic content depletes. At the present rate of degradation, only 60 years of cultivable land will be left.

Our Team The Solution

The best solution to tackle this issue:

Increasing organic content by addition of plant and animal waste is the only solution, but there is lack of encouragement within the farmers to do it. Hence, Our Website aims to provide this motive by creating a soil-centered agro forum.

How does FarmExcel Work?

The Farmers Part

The Farmers will first Register themselves in the website with details like name , address , what they grow , etc

Next Step

Then the farmers will provide the details about how much organic content is present in their soil. If the farmer finds difficulty in to find this then our soil advisors would help the farmers.

The Farmer is ready!!!

Now the farmer is ready to sell their products to traders. Now based on the organic content of the product , the cost would be decided. More the organic content, more the farmer can sell at a more price. In this way the farmer will be motivated to increase the organic content in soil.

The Trader's Part

The traders ,like farmers, will also register on our website by filling in basic details like name , traders license etc.

The trader is now ready!!!

Now the trader has access to the search index. The Search index will be included for traders where they can search particular farm products, with search filters filtering results for particular soil conditions.

The Soil Advisor's part

Now who is a soil advisor? Soil advisor is a grpoup on our website who are nothiong but people from agricultural universties across India. There work is to help farmers (online and offline both) like finding the percentage of organic matter in soil ,etc.

Registeration of Soil Advisor

There is also a registration option for people interested to becone a soil advisor.

THE TRINITY: Farmers, Traders, Soil Advisors

This website mainly connects 4 types of people which includes farmers, traders,experts and others. Our website consists of a particular feature, which sets us apart which is to include soil organic content as a parameter. Based on the organic content in soil the farmers could trade their yield. For example : The oranges grown in a soil consisting more organic content tend to have more micronutrients compared to soil of less organic content, so farmers can charge more money for the oranges grown in richer soil. This creates a motive for farmers to increase the organic content of soil. Also, people from the current generation prefer the oranges grown in richer soil. Experts act as an enzyme in our website which boosts the integrity of the system By making it easy for the farmers to communicate with them and get their issues resolved in a snap
